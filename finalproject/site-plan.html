Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote interest in local business possibilities, offer planning and guidance, create an environment in which local businesses can prosper and help businesses grow.

Audience

The chamber of commerce targets all those with talent who wish to indulge in starting a business of their own. It also accomodates those that have already started their businesses and are seeking more guidance to sustain them.
